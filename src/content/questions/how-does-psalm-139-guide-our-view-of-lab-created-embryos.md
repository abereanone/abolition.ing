Psalm 139:13-16 honors God's knitting from the first moment. Embryos made in a lab are still created by Him, and are not disposable property. Creating spares to freeze or discard treats people as products. Christians must advocate for their protection and refuse practices that commodify or destroy these little ones.

It is not loving to freeze your children, and "discarding" embryos is another euphemism for murdering them.

It's precisely because each embryo created in a lab is worthy of protection as human life that Christians must oppose this barbaric practice, not matter how sanitized our culture portrays it.
