Even after death, human remains deserve dignity because they belong to a person made by God (Deuteronomy 21:22-23).

Aborted children should not be treated as medical waste or research material, but the injustice is rooted in the legalized murder of these children, not how their remains are handled.

Christians should labor to end the bloodshed that creates this sorrow, rather than waste time and effort treating a symptom.

All that being true, it is reasonable to use the prolifer's conscience against them when discussing this. Many pro-lifers will argue that aborted fetal remains should be treated with dignity. This is a great open-door to introducing them to abolition, where the child is treated with dignity _instead_ of being murdered.
