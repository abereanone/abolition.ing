Ask: 

Does this legislation protect every child from conception without exception?

Does the bill outlaw all abortion as murder and require equal justice (Proverbs 24:11–12; Romans 13:3–4)?

Does the bill explicitly state when 'abortion' is legal, or how to do it legally? Does it refer to it as a medical procedure?

Bills that regulate murder (gestational limits, exceptions, immunity for mothers who self-abort) compromise God’s law. Support abolition bills that establish equal protection, and support these bills ONLY. Support of pro-life bills only serves to delay justice and is an abomination to our Great God.
