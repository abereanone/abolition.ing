Men who coerced abortion need conviction, confession, and the cleansing blood of Christ (1 John 1:9). Pastors should call them to repentance, require restitution where possible, and shepherd them into accountable community. Grace trains us to renounce ungodliness (Titus 2:11-12); disciplined love aims for restored, holy manhood.
